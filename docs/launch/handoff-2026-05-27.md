## Decisions awaiting your call

From `docs/launch/research-notes.md` — these are real opinions to make,
not bookkeeping:

1. **Show HN timing** — 9:30 AM PT (folklore) or **6:00 AM PT** (per
   King's April 2026 analysis of 188 K Show HN posts; 9:30 is now the
   saturation peak). My recommendation: 6:00 AM PT Tue Jul 28.

2. **Post order** — Show HN first, then T+24 cross-post to TypeDrawers
   + r/typography (research A says pre-posting risks tripping HN's
   voting-ring detection). My recommendation: flip to Show HN first.

3. **`/compare` opinion lines** — my "Vs Patens —" framings in the
   Spanish version are my words; want your eyes on those before they
   sit in production unreviewed.

4. **FAQPage JSON-LD** — research B says LLMs tokenize JSON-LD as
   raw text, don't parse it. The on-page visible Q&A is what carries
   the citation lift. `/audit` and `/help` are fine; `/pronunciation`
   has visible Q&A as prose rather than bullets — re-shape if you're
   polishing it anyway. (Optional, not blocking.)

5. **AlternativeTo + Slant + Product Hunt** listings — research B
   flagged third-party seeding as ~40% of AI citations. File AFTER
   Show HN gets you above the "≥50 stars" gate maintainers respect.
